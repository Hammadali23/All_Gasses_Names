Complete List of World Gases
Chemical Formulas and Uses
Essential Gases
Oxygen
Formula: O₂
Uses: Essential for breathing and respiration, used in hospitals for patients, welding processes, rocket fuel oxidizer, and steel production.
Carbon Dioxide
Formula: CO₂
Uses: Carbonation in soft drinks, fire extinguishers, photosynthesis in plants, dry ice production, and greenhouse cultivation.
Nitrogen
Formula: N₂
Uses: Food packaging preservation, fertilizer production, tire inflation, welding protective atmosphere, and cryogenic preservation.
Hydrogen
Formula: H₂
Uses: Rocket fuel, fuel cells for clean energy, ammonia production, metal refining processes, and hydrogenation in food industry.
Helium
Formula: He
Uses: Party balloons and airships, MRI machine cooling, deep-sea diving breathing mixtures, and leak detection.
Household & Commercial Gases
Methane
Formula: CH₄
Uses: Natural gas for cooking and heating, electricity generation, CNG vehicle fuel, and industrial heating processes.
Propane
Formula: C₃H₈
Uses: LPG cylinders for cooking, BBQ grills, heating systems, forklifts, and portable camping stoves.
Butane
Formula: C₄H₁₀
Uses: Cigarette lighters, portable stoves, refrigeration systems, aerosol spray propellant, and fuel for torches.
Toxic & Hazardous Gases
Carbon Monoxide
Formula: CO
Uses: Extremely dangerous gas from incomplete combustion, metal smelting reduction agent, and chemical synthesis intermediate.
Ammonia
Formula: NH₃
Uses: Fertilizer production, household cleaning products, refrigeration coolant, plastic manufacturing, and textile processing.
Chlorine
Formula: Cl₂
Uses: Water purification and treatment, swimming pool disinfection, paper bleaching, PVC plastic production, and chemical manufacturing.
Industrial Gases
Acetylene
Formula: C₂H₂
Uses: Metal cutting and welding, portable lighting systems, chemical synthesis intermediate, and glass blowing.
Argon
Formula: Ar
Uses: Welding protective atmosphere, incandescent light bulbs, metal production processes, and window insulation.
Sulfur Dioxide
Formula: SO₂
Uses: Wine making preservative, paper bleaching, sulfuric acid production, and food preservation.
Environmental Gases
Ozone
Formula: O₃
Uses: UV radiation protection in atmosphere, water treatment disinfection, air purification systems, and medical treatments.
Nitrous Oxide
Formula: N₂O
Uses: Medical anesthesia (laughing gas), rocket propellant, whipped cream dispensers, and automotive performance enhancement.
Hydrogen Sulfide
Formula: H₂S
Uses: Sulfur production, analytical chemistry applications, oil refinery byproduct, and geothermal energy indicator.
Noble Gases
Neon
Formula: Ne
Uses: Advertising neon signs with bright orange-red light, laser applications, cryogenic refrigeration, and voltage indicators.
Krypton
Formula: Kr
Uses: High-efficiency light bulbs, laser applications, photography flash lamps, and scientific research.
Xenon
Formula: Xe
Uses: Car HID headlights, medical imaging anesthetic, space propulsion systems, and nuclear reactor applications.
Radon
Formula: Rn
Uses: Medical radiotherapy treatments, geological surveys, earthquake prediction research, and atmospheric studies.
Chemical Industry Gases
Ethylene
Formula: C₂H₄
Uses: Polyethylene plastic production, fruit ripening agent, chemical synthesis intermediate, and welding gas mixture.
Fluorine
Formula: F₂
Uses: Teflon coating production, toothpaste fluoride compounds, uranium enrichment processes, and pharmaceutical manufacturing.
Phosphine
Formula: PH₃
Uses: Semiconductor industry doping agent, fumigation pesticide, chemical synthesis, and metal phosphide production.
Silicon Tetrafluoride
Formula: SiF₄
Uses: Glass etching processes, semiconductor manufacturing, chemical analysis, and fluorosilicic acid production.
Hydrogen Chloride
Formula: HCl
Uses: Hydrochloric acid production, metal cleaning and pickling, chemical processing, and pH control.
Specialty & Research Gases
Boron Trifluoride
Formula: BF₃
Uses: Chemical catalyst, ion implantation in semiconductors, neutron detection, and organic synthesis.
Arsine
Formula: AsH₃
Uses: Semiconductor doping, chemical vapor deposition, research applications, and analytical chemistry.
Diborane
Formula: B₂H₆
Uses: Rocket fuel component, semiconductor manufacturing, chemical synthesis, and reducing agent.
Nitric Oxide
Formula: NO
Uses: Medical treatments for pulmonary hypertension, chemical synthesis, environmental monitoring, and biological signaling.
Nitrogen Dioxide
Formula: NO₂
Uses: Nitric acid production, rocket propellant oxidizer, chemical synthesis, and atmospheric research.
Refrigerant Gases
Freon (R-12)
Formula: CCl₂F₂
Uses: Older refrigeration systems, aerosol propellants (now largely banned due to ozone depletion), and foam blowing agent.
R-134a
Formula: C₂H₂F₄
Uses: Modern automotive air conditioning, household refrigerators, commercial refrigeration, and aerosol propellants.
R-410A
Formula: CH₂F₂ + CHF₂CF₃
Uses: Home and commercial air conditioning systems, heat pumps, and refrigeration equipment.
